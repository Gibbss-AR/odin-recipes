Steak Night

Ingredients

  • 1 filet mignon from Richard's Meat Market
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 3 stems of thyme
  • 3 tablespoons of butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Set out filet for 20 minutes to room temp
  3. Head cast iron skillet until hot and add olive oil
  4. Place filet center skillet and let sit for 2 minutes to sear
  5. Flip and sit for 2 min. add tbsp of butter, garlic, and thyme
  6. Sear edges of filet for 1 minute each side
  7. Place skillet in oven for 5 minutes
  8. Let steak rest for 5 minutes
