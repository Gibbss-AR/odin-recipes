Turkey Bowl

Ingredients

  • 2lbs. Lean Ground Turkey
  • 2 boxes of rice from Aldi with roasted vegetable seasoning
  • 3 heads brocolli
  • Handful of mushrooms
  • Handful of chopped carrots
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp cumen
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Start by bringing 3.5 cups of water to a boil
  2. Once boiling, stir in rice boxes with their seasoning packets, reduce heat to simmer, time for 27 minutes
  3. Heat skillet on medium heat and add olive oil
  4. Add ground turkey to skillet, cook completely and set to the side
  5. Add all chopped veggies to the skillet, cook completely
  6. Rice should be done by this point, add everything in and portion out or portion each item separately idgaf
