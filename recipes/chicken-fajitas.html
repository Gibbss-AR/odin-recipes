Chicken Fajitas

Ingredients

  • 6 chicken breasts, sliced
  • 3 bell peppers
  • 2 onions
  • fajita seasoning
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Place chicken and veggies in separate mixing bowls
  2. add fajita seasoning and olive oil to each
  3. Air fry chicken till done
  4. Air fry veggies till done
  5. Place in containers
